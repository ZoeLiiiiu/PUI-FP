Galaxy Soho

Size: 30 x 10 in.

Galaxy Soho attracts me because of its curvy shapes and the fluidity of the inside and outside space. This drawing is a combination of multiple views from my observation of Galaxy Soho.

Hutong and Soho

Located on the boundary of the newly developed commercial area and the hutongs (the narrow streets and alleys known as the symbol of the old city of Beijing), Galaxy Soho is a landmark that labels the interesting combination of old and new.
